Welcome to Bank Staff Panel

This secure portal is dedicated to authorized bank staff only. From here you can perform various banking operations including viewing client information, processing payments, and managing accounts. Please ensure you follow all security protocols when handling sensitive client data.

Available Actions

Client Accounts

Display detailed information about the accounts of a specific client, including account status, type, and creation date.

Access Client Accounts

Payment Summary

Display the total sum of payments made by a specific client for a specified period with detailed transaction breakdown.

View Payment Summary

Account Balance

Check the current balance of a specific client's account, including available funds and pending transactions.

Check Account Balance

Make a Payment

Process a payment from a client's account to another account, either internal or external to the bank.

Initiate Payment

Card Management

Block or deactivate a client's card due to loss, theft, suspicious activity, or at client's request.

Manage Card Status
